Welcome!

In accordance with the national directives, the library is gradually resuming its activities. Therefore, in strict compliance with the instructions to avoid the spread of the Covid-19 virus, once a week, a loan service will be provided on terms adapted to the context of the current health crisis.

  • Dates: Thursday, from June 11th to July, 23rd, from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m.
  • Location: MSH Paris Nord, at the entrance.
  • Reading of documentation or archives on site will not be possible. The return of documents will not be possible.
  • All borrowings - including those made before confinement - are extended until October 19, 2020.

You can contact librarians at services.ged@campus-condorcet.fr
For more information about the situation : https://www.campus-condorcet.fr
